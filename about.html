A VISIONARY IN ARCHITECTURAL DESIGN
Troyce Quimpo, an emerging Australian architect and designer, is currently pursuing his studies at the University of Sydney. With a growing portfolio and active involvement in architectural projects, Troyce exemplifies a commitment to blending academic learning with practical industry experience. His works, such as "thecourtyardhouse_DRAWINGS" and "thestreethouse_DESIGN_BOOK," showcase his design proficiency and technical skills.
Inspiring Leadership and Community Engagement
Beyond his architectural pursuits, Troyce has demonstrated leadership and dedication through extracurricular activities. As a General Executive member of the Sydney University Catholic Society for the 2023/2024 term, he contributed to fostering community spirit at the university. His earlier achievements at Oakhill College, including a nomination for the prestigious InTech exhibition, highlight his early promise in design and innovation.
Excellence in Sports and Teamwork
Troyce's talents extend beyond the realm of architecture into sports, where he has consistently demonstrated excellence and leadership. As a basketball player, he was recognised as the Most Valuable Player for the Hills Hornets in both 2014 and 2016, showcasing his dedication to teamwork and personal growth.
