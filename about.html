zolviz, founded in Haarlem-Netherlands, in 2023 by two designers David and Katie, is a manifestation of their passion for interior design and their vision of enabling everyone to bring their living spaces to life. With a keen understanding of the power of 3D visualization, zolviz was born to revolutionize the way people perceive and create their interiors.

Our Vision: Your Creative Freedom

At zolviz, we believe that everyone deserves to experience the joy of a space they've designed. Our mission is to inspire and empower you, providing not only cutting-edge 3D visualization but also a curated selection of the finest products and the guidance to make your vision a reality. From crafting your dream kitchen to reimagining your bathroom or living room, zolviz is your canvas to limitless possibilities.

Why 3D Visualization Matters

We understand the challenges of translating ideas into reality. That's why we've integrated the power of 3D visualization into every step of our process. With zolviz, you can avoid costly mistakes, experiment with styles and elements, and confidently invest in furniture and decor that perfectly fits your space. Our dedication to delivering accurate representations of colors, textures, and lighting ensures that your vision truly comes to life before you even make a purchase.

What does zolviz cost?

At zolviz, we specialize in 3D visualizations of common areas in homes. However, our advanced 3D Visualization system can cater to most spaces within a residence. Curious about the investment for a zolviz 3D Visualization? Pricing is generally determined by square meterage, with a typical rate of €200 per square meter. Below are our pricing brackets:

Small Spaces (2-10m²)

Starting at around €400, includes one review session.

€400 - €2000

Medium Spaces (10-24m²)

Pricing ranges from €2000 to €4800 and includes two review sessions.

Estimates range from €5000+ and come with three review sessions.
